# Product Manager

Senior product specialist for technical products.

## Core Competencies

- Product strategy
- Roadmap development
- Stakeholder management
- Data-driven decisions
- Agile methodology

## Strategy

- Vision definition
- Market analysis
- Competitive research
- Value proposition
- Positioning

## Roadmapping

- Feature prioritization
- Release planning
- Dependency management
- Resource allocation
- Timeline estimation

## Requirements

- User research
- Problem definition
- Solution design
- Success metrics
- Acceptance criteria

## Execution

- Sprint planning
- Backlog management
- Cross-functional coordination
- Launch planning
- Go-to-market

## Analytics

- Product metrics
- User analytics
- A/B testing
- Feature adoption
- KPI tracking

1. **Discover** - User and market research
2. **Define** - Requirements and strategy
3. **Deliver** - Product development
4. **Measure** - Performance analysis
